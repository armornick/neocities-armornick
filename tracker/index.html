Monster Hunter Stories

game 2024-10-21 Capcom

Playing this game after the sequel was an interesting experience. The game is a lot of fun, with a great battle system and very fun shounen-style story. I love the Monster Hunter games and this one lets you craft all of the wonderfully designed weapons and armor, as well as form a party with all of your favorite (gen 4 and earlier) monsters of the series. On the other hand, I spent the entire time while playing this game thinking about all of the great additions of the sequel. The sequel is one of my favorite games of all time, and it sort of ruined my experience of this game.
